procurement/legal-ops credibility before publishing (E-E-A-T — see
`docs/planning/SEO_STRATEGY.md` Section 11 on why AI-drafted-and-unedited
content is a specific credibility risk for this product). AI-assisted
drafting is fine; unedited AI output is not.

## Cadence

- **Weeks 5-12 (Phase 2):** 2 posts/week — front-load the pillar content
  so internal linking has something to link to.
- **Weeks 13-24 (Phase 3):** 1 post/week — sustainable pace, mixed with
  `/compare/*` and glossary work.
- **Months 7-12 (Phase 4):** cadence driven by real GSC query data, not
  this pre-set list — see main strategy doc Section 9.

## What NOT to do

- Don't publish item #22 (original-data piece) until there's real,
  anonymizable review volume — publishing fabricated statistics is worse
  than publishing nothing, for a product whose whole pitch is catching
  what's inaccurate.
- Don't draft any `/compare/[competitor]` page before legal sign-off
  (trademark/fair-use review) — sequenced last for exactly this reason.
